About Dawweee

Have you ever walked the streets and wanted to pet a dog but was unable to? Well, we have the answer for you!

We provide you a destressing service by introducing you to adorable dogs around your location.

How does Dawweee work?

We provide you some puppies from your location that you may want to meet. As a result, you can make some friends (with the owner of the dog and the dog) and destress yourself. We recommend you to meet the dog(s) at public locations for your safety. If you would like to contact the owner, please use our contact form for further inquiries.
